About

Who We Are

We are the team of Arizona Aerial Productions. Born and raised here in Phoenix, we plan on giving you the means necessary to help your project or business reach its fullest potential.

Our Goal

Our goal is simple. We plan to be the best in the business for aerial media. Integrity, customer service, and quality of work are the 3 traits that separate us from the rest of the world.

Our Customer Service

We understand that integrity involves honesty with the customers and meeting agreed upon expectations. To us, customer service means being punctual with each job and having constant, accurate communication regarding each project.

Our Work

Drone technology is changing our world rapidly, and you now have the chance to stay ahead of the curve. With our expert staff and high grade technology, quality work comes naturally with our team. This allows us to give you the best product you’re looking for.

We look forward to serving you!

Services

Arizona Aerial Productions is a drone based film company for hire. We offer services to shoot any aerial film you need for any event or purpose you can think of. Call or leave us an email to find out more!

Housing & Neighborhoods

Building a neighborhood? Selling a home? Show it off with aerial photography to show a great shot of your homes and the surrounding neighborhood.

Public Events

Our expert staff will professionally capture all of the your special moments at your events. We do parties, work outings, weddings, and much more!

Movie Scenes

Shooting a movie and need an aerial scene to help show all the action? Arizona Aerial Productions can help capture all those moments to make your film perfect.
